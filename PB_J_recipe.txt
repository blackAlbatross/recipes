Ingredients:
-slices of bread of your choosing
-couple of spoons of peanut butter
-couple of spoons of jam/jelly

Method:
1. lightly toast the slices of bread.
2. on one slice of bread spread peanut butter evenly.
3. on the other slice of bread spread jam or jelly evenly.
4. combine the slices of bread together and enjoy your PB&J sandwich.

Optional:
-you cut the crust of the bread if you like.
